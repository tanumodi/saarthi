WELCOME!

At Saarthi we try to connect Ministry of Road Transport and Highways and the citizens . Innovation and simplicity makes us happy: our goal is to remove any technical or communication barriers which prevents the development . Technology has been proved to be an effective means of development. Saarthi is a solution based on GIS Based Images of the potholes/Landslides/Accidents/Other Hazards which are captured by the citizens. It connects the citizen by providing a space to report such problems by posting images of exact location. Government has the access to all the information provided and hence can resolve it as soon as possible . In return citizens do get reward points(which provides some privileges on toll-taxes etc) when a particular reported problem has been verified to be correct by the government. We're excited to connect you with the government!
